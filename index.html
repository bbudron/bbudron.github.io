Kirkland Jaxon

Producer, singer, rapper, and writer, Kirkland Jaxon is Orlando’s new rising artist. He’s had a passion for music since the beginning and has gone from playing around in his parents studio as a child to making his own music and performing on stage. Born and raised in New York, Kirkland and his family moved to Orlando, Fl at the age of 12 where he began his musical journey. Playing in a 4-part mariachi, he’s had the opportunity to perform around the country and internationally at venues such as the House of Blues, Hard Rock Cafe, and more. Now, pursuing his own career he has worked along side grammy nominated engineer's and producers. With a wide range of genre's and styles, Kirkland has the lyrical ability to paint a picture or express his views on a variety of topics including women and relationships, his view of the world, as well his hopes and plans for the future all in his own unique and original way. On his upcoming project ‘Sideshow Kirk’, Kirkland gives life to a character whom might seem no different from the average person but follows through with the dark and twisted thoughts that we all keep buried away. Things such as love, drugs and murder crosses everyones mind but Sideshow Kirk takes these vices to their extremes. From this project he has released 2 singles named ‘Lice’ and ‘Francesca’ on both which you can hear the psychotic and unpredictable personality of this ordinary yet tortured individual. With this project set to drop late September accompanied by music videos, merch, shows, etc. Kirkland stays working and creating, and has multiple projects in the vault waiting to be shared with the world.
