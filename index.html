Kirkland Jaxon

Producer, singer, rapper, and writer, Kirkland Jaxon is one of Orlando’s new rising artists. He’s had a passion for music since the beginning and has gone from playing around in his parents studio as a child to making his own music and performing on stage. Born and raised in New York, Kirkland and his family moved to Orlando, Fl at the age of 12 where he began his musical journey. Playing in a 4-part mariachi, he’s had the opportunity to perform around the country and internationally at venues such as the House of Blues, Hard Rock Cafe, and more. Now, pursuing his own career he has worked along side grammy nominated engineers and producers. With a wide range of genres and styles, Kirkland has the lyrical ability to paint a picture or express his views on a variety of topics including women and relationships, his view of the world, as well his hopes and plans for the future all in his own unique and original way. Kirkland stays working and creating, and has multiple projects in the vault waiting to be shared with the world.
